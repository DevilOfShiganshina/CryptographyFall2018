2a) Use the Euclidean algorithm to compute gcd(245873646,765384)

	245873646 / 765384 = 321
		245873646 % 765384 = 185382

	765384 / 185382 = 4
		765384 % 185382 = 23856

	185382 / 23856 = 7
		185382 % 23856 = 18390

	23856 / 18390 = 1
		23856 % 18390 =  5466

	18390 / 5466 = 3
		18390 % 5466 = 1992

	5466 / 1992 = 2
		5466 % 1992 = 1482

	1992 / 1482 = 1
		1992 % 1482 = 510

	1482 / 510 = 1
		1482 % 510 = 462

	510 / 462 = 1
		510 % 462 = 48

	462 / 48 = 9
		462 % 48 = 30

	48 / 30 = 1
		48 % 30 = 18

	30 / 18 = 1
		30 % 18 = 12

	18 / 12 = 1
		18 % 12 = 6

	12 / 6 = 2
		12 % 6 = 0

Therefore the greatest common divisor of (245873646, 765384) is, 6
